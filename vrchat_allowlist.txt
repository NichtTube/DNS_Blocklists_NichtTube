! Title: NichtTube's VRChat Allowlist
! Description: Whitelist for VRChat to override aggressive DNS blocklists. Covers core VRChat infrastructure, Photon realtime networking, Unity services, and (optional) trusted media hosts. Source: live api.vrchat.cloud/api/1/config + creators.vrchat.com docs.
! Version: 1.0.0
! Homepage: https://github.com/NichtTube/DNS_Blocklists_NichtTube
! Last modified: 30.04.2026
! Syntax: AdGuard
!
! USAGE NOTE
! All rules use @@||domain^$important so they override even high-priority
! block rules from other lists. Apply this list network-wide if you play
! VRChat on multiple devices, or per-client to your VR device.
!
! ⚠️ CONFLICT NOTE: Some entries below (*.facebook.com, *.fbcdn.net) are
! intentionally blocked by meta_quest_pro_dns_blocklist.txt. If you play
! VRChat on the Quest Pro and need Facebook-hosted video content in worlds,
! either disable those entries here or scope the Quest blocklist tightly.
!
# === Core VRChat infrastructure (REQUIRED) ===
# *.vrchat.com covers assets.vrchat.com, redirect.vrchat.com, www.vrchat.com
@@||vrchat.com^$important
# *.vrchat.cloud covers api, files, dev-assets - NOT covered by vrchat.com rule
@@||vrchat.cloud^$important

# === Photon realtime networking (REQUIRED) ===
# Master + game servers; without these you get ClientTimeout on instance join
@@||exitgames.com^$important
@@||photonengine.io^$important
